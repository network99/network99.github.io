WHAT is n99?

“Portal to THE SAFE NETWORK”

On the SAFEnetwork – the open source – decentralized internet. The network is made up by individual users who contribute storage, computing power and bandwidth to form a world-wide autonomous system.

A common wealth of human knowledge where creative people can collaborate share ideas where are rewarded for the creations they generate. n99 a network where you can shape evolution of the live decentralized data base

It’s a multimedia marketplace on the SAFE Network where you can upload your: music, videos, artwork, stories, along with many other content producers.

Once the SAFE Network reaches a certain robustness capacity provided to it by the millions of users who will be lending it memory and computer power, limits on what’s possible will be a thing of the past. Remember, the SAFE Network is a distributed autonomous network without servers. This is why data storage and computing power will not be limited at some point in the near future after there is some measure of world-wide adoption.

Also, these same facts mean that the SAFE Network will be ultra FAST!

Now, that’s all great for us the users but here’s the best part for creators:
Why should I upload my content to n99?
It will be one of the first marketplaces on the SAFE Network. Think of like a shopping mall. As the producer, you control how your content is monetized: pay-per-view, subscriptions, donations… etc.
Creators retain 99% of all SafeCoin generated by their works. n99 only retains only 1%!
That’s right. That’s the cut. 99% to artists, 1% to n99.

In the future, parents won’t ever have to tell their creative children, “be practical” or “go get a day job.” Those days are coming to an end. For this reason alone, developing this application is one of the most important things to be done with Project SAFE
What are “seeds”?
They are transferable digital tokens used in n99’s marketplace.
What are “seeds” used for?
Used to “like” content. This is good for building reputation and popularity.
Used as “gifts” exchange for: signed books, CDs, special print shirts, etc.
Used as “tickets” for events: live broadcast, 1 on 1 interview, etc.
